Electrical Safety

Basic Electrical Safety Rules

  • Always disconnect power before working.
  • Use insulated tools.
  • Wear PPE (gloves, safety glasses).
  • Verify zero voltage using a meter.
  • Follow lockout / tagout procedures.

Common Hazards

  • Electric shock
  • Arc flash
  • Short circuit
  • Ground faults
