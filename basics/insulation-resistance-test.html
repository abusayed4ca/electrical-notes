Insulation Resistance Test

Insulation resistance testing measures the condition of insulation in electrical cables, motors, and equipment.

Purpose

  • Detect insulation deterioration
  • Prevent leakage current
  • Ensure equipment safety

Test Equipment

  • Megger insulation tester
  • Multimeter

Typical Test Voltages

  • 250V – Control circuits
  • 500V – Low voltage systems
  • 1000V – Motors and cables
